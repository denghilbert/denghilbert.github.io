About Me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at Cornell University, advised by Steve Marschner. I also work closely with Paul Debevec. I received my bachelor's degree at Wuhan University. My research interests lie in Graphics and 3D Vision. During my undergraduate study, I was fortunate to work with Yansheng Li, Ming-Hsuan Yang, and Wenzel Jakob.
